What Changes
Bid invitations
never missed.

A GC's invitation to bid has a clock on it. Miss the window and you're off the job before you quoted. Intake captures and routes invitations the moment they land, so you're responding while your competitors are still checking email.

Bid follow-up
that runs itself.

Submitting the bid is half the job. The award often goes to the sub who followed up, clarified scope, and stayed reachable. Structured follow-up keeps you in the GC's mind through the award decision instead of waiting silently.

GC relationships
kept warm.

Your pipeline is a short list of GCs who invite you back. Systematic touchpoints keep you top of that list so the invitations keep coming, instead of hoping they remember you next cycle.

Project timeline
tracked to permit.

Commercial jobs move on the plan-review and permit clock. We build visibility into where each project sits so your scheduling and crew planning track the approval timeline instead of guessing at it.
