Speed

Homeowners calling for a remodel are working down a list of three or four names. They book the estimate with whoever picks up and sounds competent first. A missed call isn't a delayed lead, it's a bid that went to a competitor before you ever knew it existed.

Ticket Size

One saved job pays for the year.

Trade tickets are large enough that a single recovered remodel covers the cost of the entire system many times over. You don't need volume to justify it. You need to stop losing the jobs that are already calling you.

Time

Your job site and your phone compete, and the phone loses.

The core bind of a working contractor is that the hours you earn money are the exact hours you can't answer the phone that brings the next job. A system is the only thing that answers both at once. Hiring an office person is slower, costlier, and quits.
